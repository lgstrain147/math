Rectangles and Parallelograms

Rectangle

Quadrilateral with 4 right angles

  • Start with line AB and two other lines CD and EF which intersect line AB at right angles
  • lines CD and EF are parallel (Euclid's Elements Book 1 Proposition 27)
  • add another line GH which intersects EF at a right angle
  • ∠ CGH is also a right angle (Euclid's Elements Book 1 Proposition 29)
  • AB and GH are also parallel (Euclids Elements Book 1 Proposition 27)

Angles

  • Circular distance between (intersecting) lines
  • portion of a circle

Units of Measurement

  • degrees (Babylonians)
    • where: Circle = 360° rationale unknown but theories include:
      • ~Days in a year
      • Division of circle into equilateral triangles and then by by 60 which was the basis of their number system
  • arc length of unit circle (Euler. Elements of Algebra. 1707-1783. http://www2.clarku.edu/faculty/djoyce/trig/angle.html
  • radians

Types

  • Right Angle: equal angles formed by intersecting lines (Eculid's Elements (Book 1):Definition 10)
  • Obtuse Angle: > right angle (Eculid's Elements (Book 1):Definition 11)
  • Acute Angle: < right angle (Eculid's Elements (Book 1):Definition 12)

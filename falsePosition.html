False Position

Also known as: [1]

  • Regula Falsi
  • reckoning by (two) errors in medieval Muslim Mathematics
  • trial and error
  • guess and check

Technique [2]:

  1. Assume a answer.
  2. Perform computation based on assumption
  3. Adjust assumption based on relationship between desired result and result of computation based on assumption
  4. Verify by performing computation with adjusted assumption

Precursor to algebra. [3]

Dates back to Egyptian Rhind Mathematical Paprus problems such as: quantity problems and division of a Hekat problem 35 [4]. Division of loaves problem 40 is double false position [5].
